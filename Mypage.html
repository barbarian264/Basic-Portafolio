About me

Jorge Barbarian was born in Laredo, Tx. in December 4, 1991. In his early childhood years he was raised and lived in Nuevo Laredo Mexico, located just on the Mexican side of the border with Texas. As he grew up he enjoyed playing sports like soccer and basketball. He also learned how to play guitar and continued by learning more instruments.

As he grew up he decided to study Industrial Engineering and attended Texas A&M University starting Fall of 2010. He graduated and started working as a Production Supervisor in Food Industry.
